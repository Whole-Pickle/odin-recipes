Slow Cooker Baby Back Ribs

These are the best baby back ribs you will ever have. They take a few hours to cook but they involve a small amount of prep work. Just add these to the crockpot and forget about them until they are fully cooked.

Ingredients

  • Baby Back Ribs
  • Salt and pepper
  • water
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • BBQ sauce

Steps:

  1. Season Ribs: Season ribs with salt and pepper
  2. Add ingredients to crockpot: Add a half of a cup of water to crockpot, then add ribs. Place onions and garlic on top and cover. Let ribs cook on low for eight hours.
  3. OPTIONAL: Add BBQ sauce: Drizzle BBQ sauce in crockpot to add more bbq flavor into ribs
  4. Preheat oven: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  5. Remove ribs from crockpot: Add ribs to baking sheet and cover in BBQ sauce. Place ribs in oven for 10 to 15 minutes.

Enjoy these fall off the bone baby back ribs
