Chicken Noodle Soup

Chicken noodle soup is a quick and easy meal that anyone can make. Follow this recipe to make the best. homemade chicken noodle soup you will ever have

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles: You can use any type of pasta you enjoy. Egg noodles seem to work the best.
  • Chicken: Chicken thighs, chicken breast, or Rotisserie will work best.
  • Chicken stock: Be sure to add water to the chicken stock.
  • Vegetables: Include the staple veggies in this dish, which includes carrots, celery, onions and garlic. Feel free to add any. other veggies including spinach or kale.
  • Thyme, Bay leaves, and Parsley: These will add great flavor to the soup broth.
  1. Saute veggies: In a pot add butter along with diced vegetables. You are not looking to brown the vegetables, so stir often. Once the vegetables have started to sweat, reduce heat slightly.
  2. Add herbs and spices: Stir in Thyme, bay leaves, and garlic. Wait 1-2 minutes, so it can be incorporated with the veggie mixture.
  3. Add chicken stock: Add in chicken stock. You should allow this to simmer for a while and taste periodically.
  4. add chicken: If you plan on using a rotisserie or a precooked chicken, shred the chicken and add into broth. If the chicken needs to be cooked, ad the whole piece of chicken into broth and wait till it is cooked through. Shred chicken once cooked through.
  5. Add noodles: Add noodles last. adding the noodles of your choice last will prevent the noodles from becoming soggy.
