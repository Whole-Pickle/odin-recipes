Chili

Spice up the winter season by making your own homemade chili. This is a quick and easy recipe that require minimal effort. Also, you can add whatever you like to this dish. Follow along with this recipe and create a great family meal.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Beef Broth
  • Dried Spices
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions and Peppers
  • Beans

Steps:

Follow these steps to make an amazing chili

  1. Saute the vegetables: Remember you are able to add whatever vegetables you like, as long as you have a base of onions and peppers.
  2. Brown the ground beef: Add the ground beef to the pan and cook till brown. Remember to remove any excess fat from the pan. Once the vegetables and meet have been cooked, reduce the temp and add in the spices.
  3. Add tomatoes, broth and beans: Once these ingredients have been added, Stir all together. Allow the mixture to simmer for around 30-minutes. This will give the chili enough time to blend together.

Toppings:

  • Sour cream
  • Jalapenos
  • Onions
  • Cilantro
